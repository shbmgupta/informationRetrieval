Daulatabad, also known as Devagiri, is a 14th-century fort city in Maharashtra state of India, about 16 kilometres (9.9 mi) northwest of Aurangabad.The place was originally named Devagiri when it was an important uplands city along caravan routes, but the intervening centuries have reduced it to a village.

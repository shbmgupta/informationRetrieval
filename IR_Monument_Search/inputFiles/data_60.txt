Virupaksha Temple is located in Hampi in the Ballari of Karnataka, India. The temple is dedicated to Virupaksha, a form of Shiva. The temple was built by Lakkan Dandesha, a nayaka (chieftain) under the ruler Deva Raya II of the Vijayanagar Empire.
